As you have seen, padding is space added inside an element’s border, while margin is space added outside an element’s border. One additional differenc is that top and bottom margins,also called vertical margins, collapse, while top and bottom padding does not. Horizontal margins (left and right), like padding, are always displayed and added together. For example, if two divs with ids #div-one and #div-two, are next to each other, they will be as far apart as the sum of their adjacent margins.
